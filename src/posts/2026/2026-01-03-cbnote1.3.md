- Fixed an issue where the app might crash when opened from Camera Control at short intervals
- Fixed an issue where the camera might accidentally launch when opening the app
- Hide a shortcut that is not available on Mac
- Made some other adjustments

Update is available on the [App Store]({{ collections.projects.cbnote.links.appstore }}) and [AltStore PAL](https://i.cizzuk.net/altstore/).
