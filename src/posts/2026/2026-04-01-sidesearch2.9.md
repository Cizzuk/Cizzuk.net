- Applied the URL opening option to all assistants
- Added buttons to copy and share URLs provided by the assistant in the context menu
- Added shortcuts to change the following settings
  - Assistant
  - Start with Mic Muted
  - URL Opening Option
  - Continue in Background
  - Keep on Standby in Background
  - Sound Effects Mode
- Made some other adjustments

Update is available on the [App Store]({{ collections.projects.sidesearch.links.appstore }}) and [AltStore PAL]({{ site.links.altstore }}).
