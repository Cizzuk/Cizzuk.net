- Added the assistant status to the Live Activity
- Added an option to allow the assistant to remain on standby in the background
  - By keeping the microphone on, you can have the assistant standby in the background
  - While in standby, you can use the Side Button or Action Button to resume the assistant without opening the app
- Reduced the time until automatic speech recognition ends from 30 seconds to 10 seconds
- Enabled extending the time until automatic speech recognition ends by using Side Buttons or Action Button
- Added a shortcut to stop the assistant while it is in use
- Allowed access to audio content from other apps even while Side Search is using the microphone
- Fixed an issue where the app could crash when turning the microphone back on after the input source has been changed
- Made some other adjustments

Update is available on the [App Store]({{ collections.projects.sidesearch.links.appstore }}) and [AltStore PAL]({{ site.links.altstore }}).
