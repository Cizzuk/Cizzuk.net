- Enabled continuous speech recognition in the background even without notification permissions
- Added a setting to choose whether to continue speech recognition in the background
- If possible the assistant screen will be displayed immediately when starting the assistant from the background
- Fixed an issue where Live Activities might not start
- Fixed bugs
- Made some other adjustments

Update is available on the [App Store]({{ collections.projects.sidesearch.links.appstore }}) and [AltStore PAL]({{ site.links.altstore }}).
