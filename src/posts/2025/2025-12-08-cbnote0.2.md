- Fixed an issue where captured photos are not saved in correct orientation
- Fixed an issue where the camera preview orientation would become incorrect
- Fixed an issue where images might be displayed too large
- Added support for landscape on iPhone
- Added external camera support on iPad
- Added Japanese
- Fixed other bugs
- Made some other adjustments

Update is available on the [App Store]({{ collections.projects.cbnote.links.appstore }}).
